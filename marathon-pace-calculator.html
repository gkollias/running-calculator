Marathon Training Guidelines

Marathon Pace Training:

  • Easy Runs: 1-2 minutes per mile slower than marathon pace
  • Long Runs: Mix of easy pace with marathon pace segments
  • Tempo Runs: 15-30 seconds per mile faster than marathon pace
  • Marathon Pace Runs: Practice goal pace for 8-20 mile segments

Popular Marathon Time Goals:

  • Sub-3:00: 6:52 min/mile (4:16 min/km) - Boston Qualifier

  • Sub-4:30: 10:17 min/mile (6:23 min/km)
